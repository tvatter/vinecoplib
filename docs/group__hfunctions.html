Detailed Description

h-functions are defined as one-dimensional integrals over a bivariate copula density ,

hinv1 is the inverse w.r.t. second argument (conditioned on first), hinv2 is the inverse w.r.t. first argument (conditioned on second),

hfunc1, hfunc2, hinv1, and hinv2 mainly take care that rotations are properly handled. They call hfunc1_default, hfunc2_default, hinv1_default, and hinv2_default which are family-specific implementations for rotation = 0.
